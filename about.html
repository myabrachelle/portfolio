About Me

My name is Mya Gibson and I am a junior at the University of Michigan pursuing a dual degree in Earth & Environmental Science and Information Science, with a focus on environmental consulting. I am really passionate about balancing the needs of our community with the rising use of technology in our modern world. However, we are living in a world that is rapidly changing so solutions need to be made to address future problems. This intersection, is something that I specialize in from my previous experience in recognizing and addressing community needs through community-based companies, as well as, having the foundational knowledge in the Earth Science and what is currently occurring in our world today. I hope to work in an environment where I can bring both focuses to the table and cultivate an environment that supports the needs of both concepts.

If you want to see more of my passions then please visit my Projects page to learn more about what I have done throughout my educational and professional career!
