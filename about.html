ABOUT ME

I am really passionate about balancing the needs of our community with the rising use of technology in our modern world. However, we are living in a world that is rapidly changing so solutions need to be made to address future problems. This intersection, is something that I specialize in from my previous experience in recognizing and addressing community needs through community-based companies, as well as, having the foundational knowledge in the Earth Science and what is currently occurring in our world today.

I hope to work in an environment where I can bring both focuses to the table and cultivate an environment that supports the needs of both concepts.
