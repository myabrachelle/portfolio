About Me

I am currently a junior at the University of Michigan pursuing a dual degree in Earth & Environmental Science and Information Science, with a focus on environmental consulting and UX Design. I am really passionate about balancing community needs with rising and increasingly-changing technological advancements to create solutions that address future problems. This intersection highlights my specialization as I have previous experience in through community-based companies, coding projects and foundational knowledge developed from geological studies and projects.

In the future, I hope to work in an environment where I can bring use both skillsets to cultivate and support the needs of both fundamental concepts. Starting at a small, non-profit company, I had the opportunity to explore a role that balanced both technology and people where I developed beneficial trade skills such user needs based on the given social environment. Thereafter, I expanded my role in both the technology and geological industry through participating in various field studies in the Rocky Mountains and Ann Arbor, Michigan.

If you want to see some of my work, then please visit the Projects page to learn more about my educational and professional projects and assignments!
